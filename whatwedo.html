Our Story

WE FOR A CAUSE FOUNDATION began with one act of kindness. In 2019, our team rescued an abandoned dog named Coco. When no help arrived from other organizations, we decided to step forward and take responsibility ourselves.

That moment inspired us to build a mission dedicated to rescuing animals suffering on the streets. Today we help injured and abandoned dogs and cats receive treatment, food, shelter, and adoption support.

Our volunteers work tirelessly to create a compassionate community where animals are respected, protected, and loved.
