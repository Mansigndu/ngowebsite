MAHWAARI – Menstrual Awareness

Menstrual Hygiene and Period Poverty

Menstrual hygiene plays an important role in protecting the health and dignity of women and young girls. Unfortunately, in many rural and underdeveloped areas, lack of awareness and access to sanitary products makes menstrual health a serious challenge.

Through the MAHWAARI initiative, We For A Cause Foundation aims to spread awareness about menstrual health, break social stigma, and provide access to safe hygiene practices.

What We Do

Awareness Programs

We conduct menstrual health awareness campaigns in villages and remote communities. These sessions educate girls and women about safe menstrual hygiene practices and help remove the stigma surrounding periods.

Talk With Experts

Women can share their concerns and receive guidance from our trained volunteers and experts who provide reliable information about menstrual health and hygiene management.

Donate Hygiene Products

Support our mission by donating sanitary napkins, cloth pads, menstrual cups, and other hygiene products. Your contribution helps us distribute these essentials to women who lack access to proper menstrual care.

Join Our Initiative

Become a volunteer and help us reach communities where awareness and hygiene resources are limited. Together we can create a healthier and more supportive environment for women.
