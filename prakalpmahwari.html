MAHWAARI – Menstrual Awareness

Menstrual Hygiene and Period Poverty

Menstrual hygiene is very important for every female as this can decrease their risk of menstrual infections. Unhygienic menstruation can have adverse effects on overall sexual and reproductive health of women. And so a healthy menstruation is every woman's basic right.

We at We For A Cause Foundation will arrange awareness sessions in remote areas on menstrual health importance, and will ensure that every woman has a right to healthy menstruation and will tackle period poverty in rural areas where there is lack of sanitation and hygiene. Join us in our journey and help us arrange a menstrual hygiene session in your village for healthy menstruation.
