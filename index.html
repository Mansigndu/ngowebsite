    About We For A Cause

    We For A Cause Foundation is an animal welfare initiative dedicated to rescuing injured, abandoned, and helpless dogs and cats from the streets. Our team works passionately to provide medical treatment, safe shelter, and rehabilitation for animals in need.

    Through rescue operations, feeding drives, and adoption programs, we aim to give every rescued animal a second chance at life while spreading awareness about compassion toward animals.

    Our Vision

    To build a world where every animal is safe, cared for, and treated with kindness.

    Our Mission

    To rescue injured animals, provide medical care, promote adoption, and spread awareness about animal welfare.
