About We For A Cause

We For A Cause Foundation is an animal welfare initiative dedicated to rescuing injured, abandoned, and helpless dogs and cats from the streets. Our team works passionately to provide medical treatment, safe shelter, and rehabilitation for animals in need.

Through rescue operations, feeding drives, and adoption programs, we aim to give every rescued animal a second chance at life while spreading awareness about compassion toward animals.

Our Vision

To build a world where every animal is safe, cared for, and treated with kindness.

Our Mission

To rescue injured animals, provide medical care, promote adoption, and spread awareness about animal welfare.

Meet Our Team

The passionate people behind our mission, working tirelessly to rescue, care, and protect animals in need.

Kishor Kapdi

Director

Kishor Kapdi, a successful businessman with over four decades of experience, is the main pillar of this NGO. He is the father of Avani Kapdi. Along with his wife, Daksha Kapdi, he has been caring for animals in their village for many years. As a couple, they have consistently shown compassion towards animals and have instilled the same values of kindness and responsibility in all their children.

Avani Kapdi

Founder and CEO

Avani Kapdi, founder and CEO of the organization, is a postgraduate in Environmental Science and an experienced rescuer with 15 years of demonstrated history in non-profit management. She dreamed of starting an NGO since age 14. Her first rescue was her cat Chinu in 2006, and after 10 years of dedication, her dream became reality.

Monish Makwana

Director

Monish Makwana is a national-level athlete who used to feed community dogs and once rescued a dog named Coco without help. He started We For A Cause Foundation to ensure animals receive timely treatment and proper care, driven by compassion, integrity, and dedication.
