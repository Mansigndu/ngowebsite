    Naari Shakti

    Empowering Women is Developing Nation! We For A Cause works for women's rights and a safe, empowered environment for the transgender community because these groups are denied basic rights. We arrange campaigns to raise awareness and encourage the education of girls.

    Transgender Empowerment – The transgender community deserves dignity and respect. What you are born with doesn't matter; what counts is who you grow up to be. Particularly in India, the transgender community is mistreated. Every person deserves respect, so let's change our perspective towards them.

    Self-Defense Training – We work for women's safety because we believe every woman should be able to protect herself in an emergency. We provide free self-defense training sessions to build confidence and awareness.

    Women Empowerment – We educate women about their rights and help them develop skills for employment and independence. This reduces the risk of domestic abuse and ensures women can lead empowered lives.
