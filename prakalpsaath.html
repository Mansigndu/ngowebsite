Prakalp Saath

Project Saath is our new initiative taken up by WFC Foundation for improving the lives of children and women's by educating them and empowering them and giving poor people opportunities to earn living on their own. We focus on "Project Saath" as a vision to make our future generation independent and help our country grow on the path of development.

Mission Padhai

Our mission in this project is educating poor childrens free of cost for which we need volunteers in different areas to achieve our goal. You can donate old books, textbooks and encyclopedia for the poor children and under privileged people and help them earn a living instead of making them beg for their livelihood. Currently 15 children are taking education under Mission Padhai an we need to expand our circle more.

Mission Vastra

You can donate your old clothes and used bedsheets to give poor people in need. The torn ones would be used to make blankets and dog beds for our shelter home. We believe in reuse and recycle.No clothes will go in waste. You can donate to the needy instead of storing for years.

Mission Bhookh

Feeding is a best thing a person can do. We see lot of food getting waste in marriages and events, so Mission Bhookh is a initiative started by us. We collect food from such functions and feed it to poor persons in need instead of throwing it somewhere in gutters the food might fill the stomach of needy.
