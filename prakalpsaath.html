Prakalp Saath

Prakalp Saath is a social initiative started by the We For A Cause Foundation to improve the lives of children and women through education, empowerment, and community support. Our mission is to build a better and more independent future generation.

Through this initiative we focus on providing education, supporting underprivileged families, and promoting sustainability by encouraging people to share their resources with those in need.

Mission Padhai

Education is one of the most powerful tools for change. Through Mission Padhai, we provide free education to underprivileged children. People can donate old books, textbooks, and study materials so that children who cannot afford education still get the opportunity to learn and grow. Currently around 15 children are receiving educational support under this initiative and our aim is to expand this program further.

Mission Vastra

Mission Vastra encourages people to donate their old clothes, bedsheets, and blankets to those in need. Instead of letting clothes go to waste, we ensure they reach families who truly need them. Torn clothes are also reused creatively to make blankets and dog beds for rescued animals. This initiative promotes reuse, recycling, and compassion.

Mission Bhookh

Mission Bhookh focuses on reducing food wastage. Many events such as weddings and parties produce excess food which often gets thrown away. Our volunteers collect this surplus food and distribute it among people who need it the most. Through this mission we aim to reduce waste and ensure that food reaches those who are hungry.
