Animals often suffer because they are strays. Many people ignore their need for food, shelter, and medical care. We started We For A Cause Foundation to protect and care for these innocent lives.

So far we have rescued 500+ animals, feed 1000+ stray dogs daily, and provide medical help in cities like Mumbai, Pune, Gujarat, and Indore.
