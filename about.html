About Us

We are a passionate team dedicated to rescuing animals, spreading awareness, and creating a better and more compassionate world.

OUR STORY !!!

WE FOR A CAUSE FOUNDATION (WFC) is a NGO started in 2019 by group of friends and young volunteers who are now the change makers. Coco was the inspiration behind starting this NGO, he was abandoned and was found by us in August 2019. We tried contacting several Animal help group but no one showed up we decided to do something ourselves and we did succeed in helping Coco and saving his life. He will be always our First Rescue of WFC. Later on, we found many animals who needed help, we faced several difficulties while helping these animals and so we decided to register this NGO so that we could work on a large scale.

From a family of 3 to 50 now, We work for the well-being of animals suffering on the streets. As the name suggests, "WE" no one can bring change alone. We are constantly working towards bringing a positive change in the Environment, Society, and Lives of animals. We are here for a change. Our logo speaks to the purpose of this NGO that we work in the Environment, Animal Welfare, and also work on the social issues that are prevailing in society and we want to change the mindset of the society.

WHAT WE DO ?

As we all know that life of strays is harder and miserable. Only few care about the actual problems they face. They are born on streets survive with no shelter, no food, no care provided. We are the group of friends working together to bring peace and happiness in life of these voiceless souls. We work for a society that work together join hands with us and help us achieve our goals and make these animals life a bit better by supporting us and doing their part.

We work for women empowerment and also child education and conduct awareness campaigns where women are made aware about their rights and we arrange free self defense sessions in areas where crimes against women are high and teach the women and small girls to self defense to protect themselves. We believe everyone has a equal rights and we work in transgender empowerment.
